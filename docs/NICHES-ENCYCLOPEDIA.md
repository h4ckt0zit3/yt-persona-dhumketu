## Selection Criteria for Persona Creation

A niche is included if it meets ALL of these:

1. **Monologue/Talking-Head Format** - Creator speaks directly to camera sharing knowledge
2. **Expertise-Driven** - Content requires domain knowledge, not just entertainment
3. **Structured Knowledge** - Information can be systematically extracted and embedded
4. **Persona-Rich** - Creator develops a recognizable voice, philosophy, and framework
5. **Audience Trust** - Viewers follow for the creator's specific perspective, not just topics
